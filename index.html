We present music generation examples from WaveGAN model on the GTZAN music genre dataset without label conditioning.

This work is intended for research purposes only.

Music Augmentation

Experimentation with audio augmentation techniques were applied to the training dataset. The techniques we used aimed to both retain the key features and characteristics of the music and increase the diversity of the training dataset. We evaluated the parameters subjectively, avoiding those that appeared unnatural to a human listener. The techniques applied include time shifting, time scaling, pitch scaling, amplitude scaling, and incorporating Gaussian noise.
